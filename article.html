    Malamu Engineering - Data Science Fellowship for Research & Development. Established in 2019 Hyderabad, India with a network of experts across Africa.

    We partner with leading organizations to implement their projects in Africa. Malamu Engineering is key in taking the Smart City from Concept to Reality.

    We work where we believe the most impact can be made. Africa currently has a set of challenges and opportunities which, when solved, can impact large numbers of people including in healthcare, education, infrastructure, finance, entertainment and more.
