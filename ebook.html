MALAMU | E-Book

Helping children discover a love of reading

MALAMU | E-Book Launched in 2020, by The Knowledge Sharing Campaign (KSC) 0 an initiative of the African Institute for Mathematical Sciences (AIMS) in partnership with ThoughtWorks. MALAMU | E-Book is a library containing thousands of popular and award-winning titles that are free for children from in-need communities. The goal of MALAMU | E-Book is to encourage a love of reading and serve as a gateway to children reading even more often, whether in school, at libraries, or through other eBook reading apps..

Led by NEF Ambassador (Republic of Congo) 1, this MALAMU | E-Book is designed for many age groups and provides an opportunity for citizens to engage in science. MALAMU | E-Book will work with local partners both in the academic and private sector to make it a success.
