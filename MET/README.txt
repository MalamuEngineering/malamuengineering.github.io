MALAMU, INC. is a startup specializing in Software Design, Data Science and Digital Marketing. Our mission is to contribute to the expansion of public acceptance of the Smart City concept, in order to improve the quality of life of city dwellers. We've been working with startups, non-profits, and enterprises to produce quality software products. We have worked on building web apps, mobile native and hybrid apps, debugging and improving performance, machine learning, building an automation framework, and building data lakes. And we have worked in many language stacks like go, python, java, .net core, ruby, javascript, etc. Our approach is to be a partner. We try to understand the problem, discuss pragmatic solutions, choose a process, and deliver. We do frequent demos, take feedback, and continuously improve.
